Copotypes in DiscoElysium

There are 4 copotypes with points tracked in the journal, as well as honour points, counting for the "hidden" honour cop copotype. Earning 4 points in any given category will prompt a thought orb that (if accepted) gives Harry a corresponding thought. Whichever copotype is accepted first will be the primary copotype throughout the rest of the playthrough, however, multiple copotype

Superstar Cop

Choose dialogue that boasts about Harry's partying, mention disco, refer to him as a superstar, talk about having fans, or just generally act narcissistic and self-obsessed. After gaining 4 points, a Composure thought orb will appear and (if accepted) will afford the Some kind of Superstar thought.

Apocalypse Cop

Choose dialogue that warns of a coming apocalypse, mass extinction, or hating the world. After gaining 4 points, a Half Light thought orb will appear and (if accepted) will afford the Cop of the Apocalypse thought.

Sorry Cop

Apologize whenever possible, and choose self-deprecating dialogue. After gaining 4 points, a Suggestion thought orb will appear and (if accepted) will afford the Rigorous Self-Critique thought.

Honour Cop

After gaining 4 points, an Authority thought orb will appear and (if accepted) will afford the Overproductive Honour Glands thought.

Art Cop

After gaining 4 points, a Conceptualization thought orb will appear and (if accepted) will afford the Actual Art Degree thought.
